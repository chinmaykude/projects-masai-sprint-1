Small leaf tea is estimated to have diverged from Assam tea around 22,000 years ago while Chinese Assam tea and Indian Assam tea diverged 2,800 years ago.

Chinese leaf type tea was introduced into India in 1836 by the British in an attempt to break the Chinese monopoly on tea.
